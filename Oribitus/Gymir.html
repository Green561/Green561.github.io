History

Gymir was the 4th planet discovered in the Oribitus system. It has an extremely low population, estimated to be about 5,000. A vast majority of this consists of small tribes scattered around the island of Tiamet.

Geography

Gymir is the 3rd planet in the Oribitus system. It is estimated to be about 9% solid land. The surface lacks any major points of interest.
