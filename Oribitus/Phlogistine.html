History

Phlogistine (also known as Phlegethon) was the 2nd planet to be discovered in the Oribitus System. Located in 298 by astronomer Anetru Nupase. Due to its high temperatures and lack of natural resources, it was not a point of interest for Oribitus. However, in the early 500s, several reactors were set up near the planet's surface, transforming it into a semi-factory moon.

Geography

Phlogistine is the first planet in the Oribitus system.
