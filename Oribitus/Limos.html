History

Limos was the first planet found in the Oribitus system, Limos has an estimated population of 14.3 Billion

Geography

Limos is the 2nd planet in the Oribitus system. It is largely a desert planet. There are very few natural points of interest on the surface of the planet, however there are many man-made structures such as cities and machines. The biggest city on Limos is Talos, with a population of 24.8 million. In the center of the city, there is a Mechanica colloquially known as Augerola. Oribitus' General Research Division has classified it as MA-02.
