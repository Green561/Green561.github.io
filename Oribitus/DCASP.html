Description

The Department of Clearance Authority for Suited Personnel (DCASP) is a division of the Federation responsible for administering and regulating access clearances designated to personnel's Automated Mobility Suit. Personnel requiring changes to their clearance levels are given a reassignment card with a date to visit a DCASP facility. If they do not get their clearance level reassigned, they must collect a new reassignment card.
