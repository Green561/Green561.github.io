Biology

Humans are the perfect organisms, born to fight for Oribitus.

Limosian

A subspecies of Human native to Limos, characterized by its large, rigid head and small, sharp teeth. It possesses less developed zygomatic bones. It typically has maroon-colored skin. Its glabella is much wider than others, and it has slightly smaller eyes.

Gymirean

A subspecies of Human native to Gymir, characterized by its soft, round head and dull teeth. It possesses sunken orbital bones. It typically has burnt umber-colored skin. Its glabella is much thinner than others, and it has much smaller eyes.

Tonitrite

A subspecies of Human native to Tonitru, characterized by its hard, swelled head and dull teeth. It possesses extruded maxilla bones. It typically has taupe-colored skin. Its glabella is slightly thinner, and it has much smaller eyes

Cocytan

A subspecies of Human native to Cocytus, characterized by its wide, rigid head and many sharp teeth. It possesses hair on both of its cheeks. It typically has ivory-colored skin. Its glabella is very wide, and it has enormous eyes.
