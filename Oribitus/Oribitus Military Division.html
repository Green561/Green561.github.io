Prime Commander

A rank above Elite Commander. Responsible for keeping other Commanders in check.

Elite Commander

A rank above Commander. Responsible for overseeing all missions.

Commander

A rank above Captain. Has direct contact with Sub-Administration. Responsible for assigning missions to Omnisects.

Captain

Highest rank in both the Terrestrial and Stellar Corps. Gives orders to Generals and Chiefs.

Omnisect

26 Elite task forces that are responsible for carrying out important, small-scale missions.

General

A rank above Officer. Responsible for organizing and preparing Terrestrial Corps missions.

Chief

A rank above Leader. Responsible for organizing and preparing Stellar Corps missions.

Officer

A rank above Troop. Responsible for directing and instructing pilots during Terrestrial Corps missions.

Leader

A rank above Pilot. Responsible for directing and instructing pilots during Stellar Corps missions.

Troop

The standard rank for entry-level military personnel in the Terrestrial Corps.

Pilot

The standard rank for entry-level military personnel in the Stellar Corps.
