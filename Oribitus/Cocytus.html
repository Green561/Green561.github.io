History

Cocytus was the last planet discovered in the Oribitus System. It has a population of 2.1 billion as of 1271

Geography

Cocytus is the 7th planet in the Oribitus system. Its average temperature is -21.2° and its surface is largely composed of ice sheets and sedimentary rocks.
